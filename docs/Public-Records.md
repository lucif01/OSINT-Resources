# Public records

> Last updated: 2026-07-23 · 5 curated resources

## Introduction

Public records are strongest when sourced from the issuing authority. Requirements, availability, and privacy restrictions differ by jurisdiction; verify identity and dates before drawing conclusions.

## Resources

| Resource | Official URL | Description | Platform | Access | Open source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| OpenSanctions | [www.opensanctions.org](https://www.opensanctions.org/) | Open database linking sanctions, politically exposed persons, and other public-interest datasets. | Web/API | Free/Paid | Yes |
| ICIJ Offshore Leaks Database | [offshoreleaks.icij.org](https://offshoreleaks.icij.org/) | Investigative database of entities and people connected to major offshore-data leaks. | Web | Free | No |
| PACER | [pacer.uscourts.gov](https://pacer.uscourts.gov/) | Official US federal court-record system. | Web | Paid | No |
| Companies House | [find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/) | Official UK company register with filings and officer information. | Web/API | Free | No |
| USASpending | [www.usaspending.gov](https://www.usaspending.gov/) | Official US federal spending and award data. | Web/API | Free | No |

## Notes

Use only legitimately accessible records and comply with local restrictions on processing personal data.
